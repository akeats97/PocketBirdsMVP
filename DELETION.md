# PocketBirds Account & Data Deletion

_Last updated: May 22, 2026_

This page explains how to delete your PocketBirds account and the data associated with it.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your PocketBirds account, the following are permanently removed from our backend:

- Your account (email, password hash, display name)
- All bird sightings you've logged (species, dates, locations, notes, photos)
- All photos you've uploaded
- Your push notification token
- Your follow / follower relationships with other users

## What might be retained

In rare cases, a small amount of data may be retained for a short period to:

- Comply with legal obligations (e.g. fraud prevention, financial records — though PocketBirds collects no payment data)
- Resolve disputes or enforce our terms

Any retained data is held only as long as legally necessary and is not used for any other purpose.

## How to request deletion

PocketBirds does not currently have an in-app account deletion button. To delete your account, email:

**akeats97@gmail.com**

Please send the email from the address you used to sign up for PocketBirds, with the subject line **"Delete my PocketBirds account"**. We will confirm and delete your account within **7 days** of receiving the request.

If you cannot email from your sign-up address (e.g. you forgot which one you used), include your display name or any other detail that helps us identify your account, and we will work with you to verify your identity before deleting.

## Questions

Email **akeats97@gmail.com** with any questions about deletion or data handling. See also the [PocketBirds Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY).
