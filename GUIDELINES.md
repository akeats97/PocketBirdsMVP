# PocketBirds Community Guidelines

_Last updated: July 7, 2026_

PocketBirds is a small, friendly place to log birds and celebrate your friends'
sightings. Keeping it that way takes about four rules.

If you have any questions, email **akeats97@gmail.com**.

## The rules

1. **Be kind.** Comments, ID proposals, and usernames must not harass, insult,
   or demean anyone. Cheeky is fine; cruel is not.
2. **Keep it about the birds.** No spam, no advertising, no explicit or
   shocking content in photos, notes, or comments. Photos should be your own.
3. **Respect the birds.** Don't post content that promotes harming or
   harassing wildlife, and be thoughtful about sharing the precise location of
   sensitive or threatened species and active nests.
4. **Play the ID game honestly.** Propose identifications in good faith.
   Being wrong is completely fine (that's birding); deliberately misleading
   people isn't.

## Reporting and enforcement

- Every profile and sighting has a **Report** action (the ⋯ menu). Reports go
  straight to the maintainers and are reviewed within 24 hours.
- You can **block** any user from their profile or their content. A blocked
  user can't hoot, comment, or propose IDs on your sightings, and you stop
  seeing each other's posts.
- Content that breaks these rules gets removed; repeat or serious offenders
  lose their accounts. Objectionable-content decisions are ours to make and
  final.

## Your agreement

By creating a PocketBirds account you agree to these guidelines, our
[Privacy Policy](https://akeats97.github.io/PocketBirdsMVP/PRIVACY), and that
we may remove content or accounts that violate them. You can delete your
account at any time from the You tab, or as described
[here](https://akeats97.github.io/PocketBirdsMVP/DELETION).
