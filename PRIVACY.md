# PocketBirds Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 22, 2026_

PocketBirds is a small bird sighting logger built by Alex Keats. This page describes what data the app collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

If you have any questions, email **akeats97@gmail.com**.

## What data we collect

When you use PocketBirds, the following data is stored on our backend (Google Firebase):

- **Account info** — your email address and a password hash (used to sign you in). Optionally a display name.
- **Bird sightings** — for each sighting you log: the bird species, the date and time, an optional location label you type or select, optional GPS coordinates if you tap the locate button or pick a place from the autocomplete, optional free-text notes, and an optional photo.
- **Photos** — any photo you attach to a sighting is stored in Firebase Storage and is visible to anyone who follows you in the app.
- **Friends list** — the list of users you follow and who follows you.
- **Push notification token** — a token generated by Expo / Firebase Cloud Messaging that lets us send you a push notification when one of your friends logs a sighting. You can disable notifications in your device settings.

We do **not** collect:
- Your contacts, calendar, microphone audio, or browsing history.
- Any background location data. GPS coordinates are only captured when you explicitly tap the locate button on the Add Sighting screen, or pick a place from the autocomplete suggestions.
- Any advertising or analytics identifiers. There is no ad SDK and no analytics SDK in the app.

## How we use your data

- To show you your own bird sightings (the Field Journal and Bird Dex tabs).
- To show your sightings to friends who follow you (the Friends tab).
- To send a push notification to your friends when you log a new sighting, and to you when one of your friends logs a sighting.
- To populate location autocomplete suggestions when you type a location.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train any machine learning model.

## Who we share data with

- **Google Firebase** (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging) — hosts the backend that stores your account, sightings, photos, and push token, and delivers push notifications. Firebase is governed by Google's privacy policy at <https://policies.google.com/privacy>.
- **Expo** — relays push notifications from our Cloud Function to Google's FCM service. Expo is governed by its privacy policy at <https://expo.dev/privacy>.
- **Google Maps Platform (Places API)** — when you type into the location field, the text you type is sent to Google Places for autocomplete suggestions. Google's privacy policy applies.
- **Other PocketBirds users who follow you** — anyone who follows you in the app can see the sightings you log, including the species, date, location label, notes, photo, and approximate GPS coordinates if attached.

We do not share your email, password, or push token with any other user.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data stays in our backend for as long as you have an account. If you'd like to delete your account and all associated data (sightings, photos, follows), email **akeats97@gmail.com** and we will remove it.

## Children's privacy

PocketBirds is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has signed up, email us and we will delete the account.

## Changes to this policy

If we materially change what data we collect or how we use it, we will update this page and bump the "Last updated" date at the top.
